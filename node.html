About the apprenticeship scorecard

The apprenticeship scorecard gives data about training providers and the courses they offer. The data shows:

  • apprentice earnings five years after they complete their apprenticeships
  • the proportion of apprentices who achieve a qualification
  • learner satisfaction scores for courses and training providers

The salary data shows what people earned after they completed their apprenticeships. Other factors apart from training may affect earnings, such as previous employment history.

Prospective learners can use the data to help choose the right training for them. It currently relates only to apprenticeship training. It will be expanded to other forms of learning in future.
